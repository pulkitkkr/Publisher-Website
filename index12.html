Scientia Praecepta

Scientia Praecepta: Exploring the world of science is a series of journals of Physical Science, Chemical Science and Engineering Science and Technology consisting of Journals such as: Achievement in Material Science &Technology (AMST), Achievement in Polymer science (APS), Achievement in Nano-Science & Technology (ANST), Achievement in Magnetic Material (AMM), Achievement in Condensed Matter Physics (ACMP), Achievement in Material Modification by Radiation (AMMR) and Achievement in Computer Science & Information Technology (ACSIT), Achievement in Mechanical & Automation Technology (AMAT), Achievement in Electrical & Communication Technology (AECT), Achievement in Electrical & Instrumentation Technology (AEIT).
